What is Atlas
Combat
Full hitbox extension with size control, target hovering with radius and speed tuning, and advanced aim utilities for any fight.
Movement

Visuals
ESP, hitbox visualization, display circles, and a complete visual suite to keep full situational awareness at all times.

Pricing
Very competitively priced. Addons are cheap by design. Get a powerful base and build on it without overpaying.

Questions
Recently Asked Questions
What is Atlas and what does it do
+
Atlas is the number one Roblox script, offering a complete suite of combat tools, movement features, visual enhancements, and game-specific addons. It is actively maintained and consistently updated to stay ahead of Roblox patches.
Is Atlas safe to use
+
The team actively monitors every Roblox update and pushes fixes quickly. While no script can guarantee zero risk, Atlas is built with longevity in mind and has a strong track record of staying operational through patches.
How much does Atlas cost
+
Atlas is priced to be very accessible. The base package is cheap and addons on top of it are even cheaper. You only pay for the games you actually play rather than a bloated all-in-one bundle.
What games does Atlas support
+
Atlas currently supports Loomian Legacy, Arsenal, Rivals, Speed Run 4, and Deepwoken in beta. The games list continues to grow as new addons are developed. Check the Games page for the full current list.
How do I get Atlas
+
Join the Atlas Discord for purchase links, setup guides, and live support. The team will walk you through everything from purchase to getting the script running in game.
What are addons and how do they work
+
Addons are low-cost extension packs that layer game-specific features on top of the Atlas base. Instead of one expensive package, you pick the games you play and only pay for those addons.
